PLAGIARISM POLICY

Plagiarism is the unethical act of copying someone else’s prior ideas, processes, results or words without explicit acknowledgement of the original author and source. Self-plagiarism occurs when an author utilizes a large part of his/her own previously published work without using appropriate references. This can range from getting the same manuscript published in multiple journals to modifying a previously published manuscript with some new data.

All manuscripts submitted for publication through Labtech Innovations are cross-checked for plagiarism using Turnitin/ithenticate software. Manuscripts found to be plagiarized during initial stages of review are out-rightly rejected and not considered for publication in the journal.

PhD Thesis Defence Preparation

Preparing for the defence session of your thesis is considered to be a daunting task. Even if you have put in all your best efforts towards submitting an absolutely brilliant PhD Thesis, it becomes quite frightening when it comes to facing the defence of your dissertation.

To do list for to prepare for your PhD defence.

☑Keeping the answers as simple and precise as possible to the questions raised by the committee
☑Engaging into the questions honestly without trying to manipulate the committee
☑ Having all the material that you need to answer the questions satisfactorily, like data sheets, images and graphs
☑Knowing the background references and citations in-depth which have been used in PhD thesis

☑Lastly, never being afraid to say “I don’t know”

Systematic Literature Review

Systematic Literature Review (SLR) can be seen as a step ahead taken in literature review. It also searches, identifies, selects and then analyses the existing literature to answer the questions raised in the current research work. However, the critical approach is enhanced in a systematic approach to fulfill the purpose of providing better credibility and clarity. This rigorous process does not just involve finding the accurate literature for the study but also strategically planning the methods of collecting those studies. The criteria and approach of your study also remain the focal point while conducting the SLR, as it is done with the purpose to improve the existing knowledge about the discoveries in the topic area, to evaluate the research methodologies that have been used earlier and to come up with better theories and analysis.

PhD Synopsis Writing Service

PhD Synopsis or Thesis Proposal is considered to be the most important step for the development of a successful thesis because if it gets rejected, you can’t move on to the process of developing a thesis. Therefore, it becomes crucial to prepare a synopsis that highlights the significance of your proposed research work. Along with the research topic and title, the synopsis must include a brief introduction, scope and purpose of the research, the chosen research methodology, presentation of the findings in support of your research, questions which will be answered, time needed for the research and appropriate resource citations to show the credibility of your research.

Conceptual Framework Design

Conceptual Framework or Research Framework can be defined as the illustrations which are used to highlight the important variables which show the findings from the research. Most of the time, these frameworks are based on the literature review of the research. It most importantly shows the cause and effect relationship between the dependent and independent variables. This simple structure can become trickier with a large number of variables affecting the data and when there are other variables like moderating variables, mediating variables and controlling variables. Therefore, it becomes important to draw a conceptual framework in an illustrated graphic for a better clarity.

Benefits of Conceptual Framework

👍The visual framework of research can be used for both theoretical and statistical research methodologies.
👍 It helps in visualising the plan and strategizing process of the research as well.
